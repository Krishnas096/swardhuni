SWARDHUNI

An Autmomated water quality Monitoring and Flow Diverting System(AWMFDS)

Sensors are placed on the route from industry waste exit point to the water body. If the waste water released is less harmful, project model allows it to go to water body. Sensors check the nature of waste water released and send data to the server. This data is also used to decide the flow of the waste water.

Our Mission

Monitor

Regular monitoring of quality of water bodies using an automated system and provide a safe and clean ecosystem for both the People and the Marine life.



Its Importance

Aquatic ecosystems are an integral part of environment. They need to be maintained as the world conservation strategies stress the importance of maintaining ecosystems and genetic diversity.

Maintain Transparency

Water bodies often go unregulated regarding dumping of sewage and harmful excreta in many places in India. Our System makes it easy to monitor the quality of water bodies by both the Government and the General Public.

Global Awareness

Make our System Scalable so that it helps in saving large number of Water bodies globally and safeguarding it from Contamination by uncontrolled sewage discharges and pollution.
